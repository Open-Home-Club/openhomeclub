# Open Home Club

Open Home Club is an experimental, non‑commercial platform for people
who want to share space, mobility and local support outside the logic
of the traditional market economy.

This project is built around reciprocity, trust and direct human agreements —
not payments, platforms or optimization.

## Why this exists

Many people travel, move or live between places.
Not everyone wants hotels, short‑term rentals, paid services or rigid platforms.

Open Home Club exists for those who prefer:
- mutual help instead of transactions
- barter instead of pricing everything
- contribution instead of consumption
- relationships instead of rankings

This is not about “free stuff”.
This is about **alternative forms of exchange** that already exist —
but often have no place online.

## What can be found here

- offers and requests for shared living space
- non‑commercial sharing of vehicles
- local support / helping hand from people on the ground
- temporary arrangements based on trust and transparency

There are no payments, no commissions and no intermediaries.

All arrangements are made directly between people.

## What this is not

- not Airbnb
- not Booking
- not a marketplace
- not a job platform
- not a service directory

Open Home Club does not verify users
and does not take responsibility for agreements between people.

## Language

The interface is in Polish.
All listings can be automatically translated into European languages.
