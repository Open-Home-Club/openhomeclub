# Open Home Club

Open Home Club is a **non-commercial, community-driven platform** for sharing homes, vehicles, and local support — outside short-term rental and licensing markets.

The project exists for people who:
- own underused spaces or vehicles,
- are excluded from commercial rental platforms by regulations,
- prefer reciprocity and cost-sharing over transactions.

## What this is
- A classified board for offering and looking for:
  - shared spaces,
  - shared mobility,
  - local, human support.
- No payments, no commissions, no intermediaries.

## What this is not
- Not Airbnb or Booking.
- Not a rental service.
- Not a marketplace or job platform.
